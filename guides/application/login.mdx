## Sign in

1. Open the application and choose your sign-in method
2. Complete authentication and you'll land in your organization

## Switch organizations

If you're a member of multiple organizations, use the org switcher from the header.

## Troubleshooting

- Invited but can't log in: ensure you accepted the invite link
- Access denied: ask an admin to add you to the correct organization
- Stuck on loading: refresh and try again
